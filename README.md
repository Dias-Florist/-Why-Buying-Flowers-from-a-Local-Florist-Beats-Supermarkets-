## 💡 The Hidden Problem with Supermarket Flowers

Supermarket flowers may look appealing at first glance.

They’re affordable.  
They’re accessible.  
They’re ready to go.

But what most people don’t see is the process behind them.

✔ Mass-produced in bulk  
✔ Transported over long distances  
✔ Stored before reaching shelves  

By the time you buy them, they are often already a few days old.

This directly affects how long they last, how they open, and how they look after just a short time.

What seems cheaper upfront often delivers less value overall.
